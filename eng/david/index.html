15:1 GOD, who may so­journ in your tent? Who may dwell on your ho­ly hill? 2 He who walks with in­teg­ri­ty and does what is right and speaks truth in his heart; 3 who does not slan­der with his tongue and does no evil to his neigh­bor and does not take up a re­proach a­gainst his friend; 4 who despises a vile per­son but hon­ors those who fear GOD; who keeps a prom­ise e­ven when it hurts and does not change; 5 who does not loan out his mon­ey with in­ter­est and does not take a bribe a­gainst the in­no­cent. He who does these things shall nev­er be moved.

19:1 The heavens de­clare the glo­ry of God and the skies show his hand­i­work.

The com­mand­ment of GOD is pure, enlightening the eyes. 9 The fear of GOD is clean, en­dur­ing for­ev­er. The laws of GOD are true and right­eous al­to­geth­er.
