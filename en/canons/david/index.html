Proverbs of David

A selection of proverbs taken from the Book of Psalms in the Hebrew Bible. Verse references such as 12:8 indicate the original source of the text, but donʼt imply a full quotation.

2:11 Serve the Lord with fear and rejoice with trembling.

4:4 In your anger, do not sin. Ponder in your heart on your bed and be still. 5 Offer the sacrifices of righteousness and put your trust in the Lord.

12:8 The wicked walk on every side when vileness is exalted among the children of men.

15:1 Lord, who may sojourn in your tent? Who may dwell on your holy hill? 2 He who walks with integrity and does what is right and speaks truth in his heart; 3 who does not slander with his tongue and does no evil to his neighbor and does not take up a reproach against his friend; 4 who despises a vile person but honors those who fear the Lord; who keeps a promise even when it hurts and does not change; 5 who does not loan out his money with interest and does not take a bribe against the innocent. He who does these things shall never be moved.

18:25 The Lord shows mercy to the merciful.

18:30 The word of the Lord proves true. He is a shield to all who take refuge in him.

19:1 The heavens declare the glory of God and the skies show his handiwork.

19:7 The law of the Lord is perfect, reviving the soul. The testimony of the Lord is trustworthy, making wise the simple. 8 The precepts of the Lord are right, rejoicing the heart. The commandment of the Lord is pure, enlightening the eyes. 9 The fear of the Lord is clean, enduring for ever. The laws of the Lord are true and righteous altogether.

27:14 Wait for the Lord. Be strong and let your heart take courage. Wait for the Lord.

30:5 Weeping may last for a night, but joy comes in the morning.

32:10 Many sorrows come to the wicked, but loving-kindness surrounds him who trusts in the Lord.

33:6 By the word of the Lord the heavens were made and all their host by the breath of his mouth.

34:13 Keep your tongue from evil and your lips from speaking deceit. 14 Depart from evil and do good. Seek peace and pursue it.

37:1 Fret not because of evildoers, and envy not wrongdoers.

37:7 Be still before the Lord and wait patiently for him. Fret not because of him who prospers in his way; because of him who carries out wicked schemes. 8 Cease from anger and forsake wrath. Fret not—it leads only to evildoing.

37:16 A little that the righteous has is better than the abundance of many wicked.

37:21 The wicked borrows and does not pay back, but the righteous is generous and gives.

39:1 I said, I will guard my ways so that I may not sin with my tongue. I will muzzle my mouth while the wicked are in my presence.

41:1 Blessed is he who considers the poor. The Lord will deliver him in the day of trouble.

46:1 God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble.

46:10 Be still and know that I am God.

49:16 Do not be afraid when one is made rich, when the splendor of his house increases. 17 For when he dies he will carry nothing away. His glory will not descend after him.

50:14 Offer to God a sacrifice of thanksgiving and pay your vows to the Most High 15 and call on me in the day of trouble. I will deliver you and you will glorify me.

55:22 Cast your burden on the Lord and he will sustain you. He will never allow the righteous to be moved.

82:3 Defend the poor and fatherless. Do justice to the afflicted and destitute. 4 Res­cue the poor and needy. Deliver them from the hand of the wicked.

92:12 The righteous will flourish like the palm tree. He will grow like a cedar in Lebanon.

96:2 Sing to the Lord, bless his name. Proclaim his salvation from day to day. 3 De­clare his glory among the nations, his marvellous deeds among all the peoples.

100:1 Make a joyful noise to the Lord, all the earth. 2 Serve the Lord with gladness. Come before him with singing.

102:17 The Lord regards the prayer of the destitute and does not despise their prayer.

103:8 The Lord is merciful and gracious, slow to anger, abounding in loving-kindness.

105:1 Oh give thanks to the Lord. Call on his name. Make known his deeds among the peoples. 2 Sing to him. Sing praises to him. Talk about all his marvelous deeds. 3 Glory in his holy name. Let the hearts of those who seek the Lord rejoice. 4 Seek the Lord and his strength. Seek his face continually. 5 Re­mem­ber the wonders that he has done, his miracles and the judgments he has pronounced.

106:1 Praise the Lord. Oh give thanks to the Lord, for he is good; for his loving-kindness endures forever.

111:10 The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom. Those who obey his commandments have good understanding.

112:1 Blessed is the man who fears the Lord, who delights greatly in his commandments.

112:4 For the upright, light arises in the darkness. He is gracious, merciful and righteous. 5 It is well with the man who deals generously and lends, who conducts his affairs with justice.

118:8 It is better to take refuge in the Lord than to put confidence in man. 9 It is better to take refuge in the Lord than to put confidence in princes.

122:6 Pray for the peace of Jerusalem.

127:1 Except the Lord build the house, they labor in vain who build it. Except the Lord keep the city, the watchman stays awake in vain. 2 It is vain for you to rise up early, to stay up late, to eat the bread of toil—for he gives sleep to his beloved.

127:3 Behold, children are a heritage from the Lord and the fruit of the womb is a reward.

133:1 Behold, how good and pleasant it is for brothers to live together in unity.

136:1 Oh give thanks to the Lord, for he is good. His loving-kindness endures for ever.

146:3 Do not put your trust in princes or in the son of man, who cannot save.
